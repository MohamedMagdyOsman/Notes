## 2ï¸âƒ£ Brief History: Linux vs Unix

### Unix (1970s)
- Created at **Bell Labs**
- Proprietary
- Used in enterprises and academic institutions
- Expensive and closed-source

### Linux (1991)
- Created as a **Unix-like** alternative
- Free and open-source
- Community-driven
- Compatible with Unix principles

### Key Differences

| Linux | Unix |
|-----|------|
| Open-source | Mostly proprietary |
| Free | Usually paid |
| Community-driven | Vendor-controlled |
| Highly customizable | Limited customization |

> Linux is **not Unix**, but it is **Unix-like**.
